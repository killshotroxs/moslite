Privacy Policy – Pet Boba

Last Updated: February 2026

Pet Boba ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Pet Boba mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

A. User-Provided Content

  • Images you voluntarily upload for AI artwork generation
  • Prompts or customization inputs you submit

B. Generated Content

  • AI-generated images created from your uploads
  • Associated metadata (generation timestamp, usage count)

C. Device and Usage Information

  • Device identifiers
  • App interaction data
  • Session information

D. Subscription Information

  • Subscription status and transaction state via Google Play Billing
  • We do not store your full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Generate AI-based pet artwork upon request
  • Store and display your generated images
  • Provide subscription features
  • Improve application performance
  • Prevent abuse and maintain security

We do not use your images or data to train AI models.

3. Data Storage and Processing

  • Uploaded images are processed through our AI generation pipeline.
  • Generated images and related metadata are securely stored using Convex, a backend service provider.
  • We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect stored data.

4. Data Retention

Generated images remain stored until:

  • You manually delete them, or
  • You request account/data deletion.

Uploaded images are retained only as necessary to complete processing.

We do not retain data longer than necessary to provide the service.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Upon verified request:

  • All generated images associated with your account will be permanently deleted from our Convex database.
  • This deletion is irreversible.
  • Backup copies, if any, are removed according to standard system retention cycles.

To request deletion:

  • Use the in-app settings (if applicable), or
  • Contact us at: petboba@krishnal.one

Deletion requests are processed as soon as reasonably possible.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing – subscription management
  • Convex – secure database and backend infrastructure
  • Cloud AI processing providers – for image generation
  • Analytics providers – if applicable

These providers process data solely on our behalf.

7. International Data Transfers

  • Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence.
  • We take reasonable measures to ensure data is protected consistent with this Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

Pet Boba is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccuracies
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent

Contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Security

We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at the Privacy Policy URL.
