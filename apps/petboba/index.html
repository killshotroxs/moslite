Pet Boba – Data Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data

At Pet Boba, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time, either directly through the app or by contacting us.

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete all your data directly from within the Pet Boba app by following these steps:

In-App Deletion Steps

1
Open Pet Boba

Launch the Pet Boba app on your device

2
Go to Settings

Navigate to the Settings screen

3
Find Legal Section

Scroll down and tap on Legal

4
Select Delete My Data

Tap on Delete My Data option

5
Confirm Deletion

Tap the red Delete My Data button to confirm

Important Warning

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once you delete your data, all generated images will be permanently removed from both your device and our servers.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, the following information is processed:

Data Type Status
Generated AI Images Deleted
Uploaded Pet Photos Deleted
Image Metadata (timestamps, etc.) Deleted
App Usage Data Deleted
Device Identifiers Deleted
Subscription Status Retained*

*Subscription status is managed by Google Play and retained for billing purposes. You can manage your subscription separately through the Google Play Store.

Alternative: Contact Us

If you prefer, you can also request data deletion by contacting us directly. We will process your request as soon as reasonably possible.

Data Retention

Backup copies of deleted data, if any, are removed according to our standard system retention cycles. We do not retain your personal data longer than necessary to provide our services.
